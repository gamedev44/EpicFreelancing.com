📖 Summary

We're seeking a freelance animator to create first-person combat animations that also look clean from a third-person camera. You'll be working with a full-body UE5.5 skeleton, but animation is focused strictly on the arms and upper body.

🔧 Scope of Work

  • ✅ Shoulders
  • ✅ Upper Arms
  • ✅ Elbows
  • ✅ Lower Arms
  • ✅ Hands & Fingers
  • 🧠 (Rarely) Head / Neck

No legs, hips, or full-body motion unless contracted separately for advanced moves like flips or dashes.

🎯 Animation Types Needed

  • Weapon Idle / ADS / Firing Poses
  • Reload Sequences
  • Grenade Throws & Power Gestures
  • Force/Magic-style Attacks (Skyrim-inspired)
  • General Combat States & Transitions

🛠 Tools & Delivery

  • Blender preferred, using the Dopesheet Action Editor workflow
  • Maya is also fully supported — we have licensed access if needed
  • Output clean, FBX-ready animations (UE5.5-compatible)
  • No UE5 importing/retargeting needed — we handle that

    You Must:

  • Ensure bone chains remain unmodified
  • Ensure any bones Besides Arms and Neck or Head remain Absolutely unmodified as to not Break our Current Workflow

We’ll provide:

  • Full-body skeleton (for use, not full-body animation)
  • Meshes for weapons or effects as needed
  • Git/Repo access or upload location for animation files

💰 Payment

  • $10 USD per completed, approved animation (arms-focused)
  • Higher pay available for full-body combat animations (e.g. flips, rolls, etc.)
  • Payment made after successful testing and approval
  • Payments via PayPal, Remitly, Patreon, or Fiverr

✅ Ideal Candidate

  • Experienced in FPS-style animation (arms-focused)
  • Confident using Blender or Maya
  • Understands how to Uncheck Keep Feather Bones Amongst other import/export options in blender when using UEFN manny
  • Understands UE5.5-compatible pipeline delivery
  • Good with modular, game-ready motion (no over-animating)
